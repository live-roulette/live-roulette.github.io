How this European roulette demo works

The game uses a European roulette wheel with numbers from 0 to 36 and the real casino number order on the rim. The wheel layout is single-zero only, which means a lower house edge compared to American roulette with 0 and 00.

For bets, the demo supports popular inside and outside options:

  • Straight number (e.g. 17): pays 35:1.
  • Red / Black: pays 1:1.
  • Even / Odd: pays 1:1 (0 loses).
  • Low (1–18) / High (19–36): pays 1:1 (0 loses).
  • Dozens (1–12, 13–24, 25–36): pay 2:1.

All results are generated in the browser. When you press Spin, the game randomly selects a pocket on the European wheel, animates the ball towards that segment, and shows the winning number both on the table and directly in the game window.

Balances and payouts are simulated locally. There is no real-money play, no registration, and no connection to external websites — this is a standalone online roulette simulator.
